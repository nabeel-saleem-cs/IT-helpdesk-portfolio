\# Email Issues



\## Common Problems

\- Outlook not syncing

\- Emails going to spam

\- Cannot send emails



\## Troubleshooting Steps

1\. Verify internet connection.

2\. Restart email client.

3\. Check account configuration.

4\. Reconfigure profile if needed.

5\. Verify SMTP/IMAP settings.



\## Spam Problems

1\. Check spam filter.

2\. Whitelist trusted contacts.

3\. Review mail rules.



\## Outcome

Email functionality restored.
